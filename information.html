What is Project Grindstone?

Project Grindstone is an ARG involving in-depth lore and extreme world-building. All the members are applicants to the Universal Magical Academy at Dial (UMAD), an extraplanar government entity set to increase their understanding of our plane while also exchanging their information. 

Players interact with each other, solving challenging plot-based puzzles of many types and collaborating in a community to contribute to the main arc of the story. New players are tasked to complete different ciphers and puzzles to advance in the game and receive more dangerous missions. 

The goal of the ARG is to be accepted as a full member of The Universal Magic Academy at Dial, as to be let in on all the secrets their organization is hiding.

Common Questions

What are Departmental Assignments?

Departmental Assignments are a way for UMAD to collect information on our “world.” In exchange, those who report in a timely manner are awarded DPC which are then applied to earning a position in UMAD after the recruitment period. Departmental Assignments are given every Tuesday and Thursday in #server-events

What are Extra Competency Exams?

These are 3-stage assignments you'll be given to test you Competency, those who complete an entire exam will we awarded 50DPC. If you wish to ask for a hint, It will cost 25% of the total DPC awarded.

What is DPC?

DPC is one of the factors consider as a part of your applicant scorecard, if you score well on your applicant scorecare you may be offered to choose your own department, otherwise you will be assigned to a department as UMAD sees fit. Your DPC is also used to measure how engaged you have been within the community and it's events. Currently your are unable to access your DPC score, but this may change in the future after the application process.
