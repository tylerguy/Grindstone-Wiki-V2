Welcome to the Project Grindstone Wiki!

Project Grindstone is an Alternate Reality Game (ARG) involving in-depth lore and world-building, where all members are applicants to UMAD, the Universal Magical Academy at Dial.

The depth of information might be intimidating, but not to worry! A majority of information are things you will learn with time, and there's no need to memorize any of it right immediately. The Wiki serves as an ever-evolving database, but the best way to learn is experiencing. Don't hesitate to ask questions at any point about anything.

We're glad you're here and hope you enjoy your time with us at UMAD!
