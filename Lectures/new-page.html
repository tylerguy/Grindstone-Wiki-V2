Degradation

After one has accomplished creating a system of programming, a construct, it should be noted that this system is not perfect. For several reasons constructs will degrade over time and require maintenance. Sorry for anyone who wants to save up a small fortune to buy a large metal assistant but you'll need to keep shelling out more money to get that assistant to continue to function or else learn how to program on your own. Degradation occurs for several reasons but there are measures that can slow down the progression of degradation in a construct. For instance, if the construct has fine tuned points of aeonization you can limit interference thus minimizing damage to the frame itself. Lets break down what these phrases mean however. 

As you know the frames are almost like skeletons of aether not normally visible to the unaided eye and require contingent casting by the programming in order to continue to draw aether to maintain their presence. It is for this reason we call all frames dynamic. The aether present in the frames in a moment is not the same aether present in a frame in the next moment. Since constructs are constantly "casting" on themselves they are very visible with simple aeonization, some of the largest and most dense constructs can be visible from far distances if an aeonization expert is present in fact.

On the Elementary Components of Theoretical Self Aetherial Programmed Spellcasting is a a text on how it is possible that aetherial mechanisms can themselves utilize the aether if programmed to - but this is impractical due to the maintenance required in programming. There has never been a perfectly non-degrading construct. Sometimes when the construct aeonizes and summates aether it creates something called interference. Interference is one of several things that cause degradation. Interference occurs when the aether summated crosses the frame at a point disrupting its structure. With enough interference minor lines of programming might dissipate with unintended directions or complete interruption much like how mutations occur in genetics. Interference occurs because it is impossible to adjust a contruct to predict aetherial wave patterns with perfect accuracy thoughout tim(sic).
