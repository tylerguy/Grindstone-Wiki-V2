Aeromatics

Aeromatics are exactly what one would suspect. Alchemical compounds that freely present in the air without visible volatility and contain a strong smell. The perception of these scents seem to differ from certain species and so it is suggested that they directly effect the physiology of species differently. Aeromatics of stronger scent are believed to be larger in size and have more area to buffer other scents. They also aggregate with one another. It is for this reason that one can still become ill when around the diseased air even in the presence of excess medicinal aeromatics for their safety. The likelihood of imbalance is simply decreased.

Medicinal aeromatics are large aeromatics that have little effect on one's physiology making it difficult to create an imbalance. This typically only happens in very large quantities leading to the resulting theory that anything in the right amount can cause an imbalance. This is a theory of virtue health.
