Central Territories

West of Central Belthas is Oudste. This place occupies the majority of the major mountain ranges but also plenty of neighboring land. While not the best description we approximate the mountains in this region to making something of a y shape. The country of Oudste has many contested areas but has commonly accepted boarders between it and neighboring countries. Oudste is also a country with many settlements found in the sub-terra as well as above ground with "tolerated" settlements that are not officially recognized Oudstian and are governed with unique laws to the settlement. The town government of settlements in Oudste are some of the most varied. Due to its unique position the government regulates trade with fierce tariffs which many trade caravans accept for scheduled protected travel periods through the sub-terra of the mountains instead of trading through the loop, the bitter North, or deal with radicals and the blightlands to the South.

Oudste has two capital cities of the same name but most of their governing entities reside in the East and not the West Donderspleet. The aforementioned y shape has a longer North West arm and a hooked tail. While Oudste claims much of the land in between the North West arm's tip to the tail of the mountains due West of Oudste are nine separate nations. This will be a little more difficult to explain the positions of these nations so please do not hesitate to ask questions.
