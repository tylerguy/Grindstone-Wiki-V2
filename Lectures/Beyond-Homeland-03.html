After a slight delay I am here for a final lecture until processing has concluded.

Today we will be finishing the gross geography of the continent where Belthas is located. After this we will discuss landmarks and unique geography but I highly encourage questions as I know there is not nearly enough time to cover all existing major landmarks. We will focus on places of interest decided by you.

I understand there was some confusion last lecture about the boarders of Victoria. I will clarify this today as well.

Victoria

Much like Belthas there is a peninsula, although much less pronounced, in the far South West within Victoria that has the most Western settlement on the continent Bordeaux which at the edge of town the constant storm can sometimes be seen. Seven of the central territories, The North, and Oudste all can be found on the boarder of Victoria. Victoria and Belthas are the two largest known countries.

The capital of Victoria is located North of the Wetlands Latitude just north of Tranquil on the edge of the largest lake Victoria has total ownership of. To the far North West however is the largest of the lakes in the continent with a small collection of islands two of which have dense forests and are claimed by Victoria but the Northern waters are often contested.

The Blightlands

The Blightlands four major nations but due to radical factions the exact boarder is often contested. This place is rich in rivers and has several smaller major lakes scattered within. For now these nations will remain unnamed however the far west nation is the smallest and is bordered by three central territories with the farthest South Eastern central territory also being part of the boarder to the most central and largest of the nations in the Blightlands. The border of these two nations are created by a major river that feeds into one of the smaller major rivers that is shared by the South Eastern most central territory and the two nations aforementioned in the Blightlands. Rivers also form boarders with the Eastern most, central, and Southern most nations. The southern most nation forms a boot like shape often called the Cuff and is where much trade with the blightlands takes place through the loop. North of the cuff is a large island that hosts two recognizable settlements with a sister island directly to its South West. (edited)

The Eastern Nation shares a boarder with Belthas with another major lake that takes a fat crescent shape with small collections of islands within. North West of the Eastern Blightlands nation is a place the blight hardly effects. This place is referred to as No Mans Land and Belthas does not recognize any owner of this land.

We will at this time not elaborate on the Far North and thus conclude gross geography of the continent.
