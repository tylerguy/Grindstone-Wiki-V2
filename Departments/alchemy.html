Aeromatics

  • Alchemical compounds that freely present in the air without visible volatility and contain a strong smell
    • Stinky, colorless
  • A scent is perceived differently per species, suggesting it effects the physiology of a species differently 
  • Stronger scent is believed to be larger in size and have more area to buffer other scents
