Gillie

On November 17th, 2021 Gillie sent a message congratulating Applicants and assuring everyone their efforts are worthwhile.

Transcript

To the latest group of Applicants to the Universal Magical Academy of Dial, I'd just like to say congratulations for those of you who have already been accepted and good work to all of those who are still working through admissions. I know that this time has been difficult for many of you, in many ways. The tests can be daunting and the lectures somewhat dull and dense. But I promise it's all worth it in the end. You are all on a path that not many have the opportunity to take. You have a chance join the ranks of UMAD and work alongside Researchers like myself, to unravel the mysteries of not just our world and plane but many, many others. The work can be dangerous and difficult, and it is for that reason that we must all undergo such rigorous testing in order to gauge how well equipped we are to the task. The safety of not just yourself but those around you could very well depend on what you know and how you're able to handle yourself under the pressure. Some of you have already shown you are prepared enough to begin your duties as Research Assistants through careful examination of your knowledge and your character. Others who have not had the chance to undergo such exhaustive consideration will be beginning as Employees within UMAD. With the chance to become promoted to Research Assistants in the future. Please, do not consider this a penalty or an indication that you are somehow lacking something essential. I myself began my career within UMAD as an employee, and I do not consider it a source of shame. I've made a good home for myself here, with plenty of friends among my colleagues. And over the years I've risen steadily through the ranks to become a Research Assistant as well. At present I am even under consideration for promotion to Researcher. Your works will be rewarded and the struggles so far will not have been in vain. I look forward to our time together. Thank you.
