Notable Locations

Spawn

Eastern City

Description

The Eastern City is an abandoned city filled with empty houses, structures, statues, gardens, arenas, and shops. It contains three shops where players may sell a small selection of items to obtain in-game currency. It also contains lecterns with server updates and information, as well as other information about the city. On each outskirt of the city stands a wide watchtower-esque structure.

The Eastern City is accessible to players through the command /warp EasternCity.  

Images

Arena

Description

The Arena is a location within the Eastern City where players will presumably be allowed to test their strength through battle. It is currently non-functional, however players will receive an automated message upon approaching it. The building has two tiers, and both sides contain a balcony with chairs overlooking the Arena. Barrier blocks prevent entry into the Arena floor from above. 

The Arena can be located at the coordinates (1497, 2477).

Temple

Description

The Temple is a large circular structure near the center of the Eastern City. It has four entrances, all barricaded by amber blocks. A small hole now allows entry into the Temple, which contains soul fire and a pillar of netherite blocks surrounded by empty lecterns. 

The Temple can be located at the coordinates (1592, 2370).

Aquarium

Description

The Aquarium is a small glass case containing water and coral with a few small tables seated in front of it. In the center stands a lectern containing a book. 

The Aquarium can be located at the coordinates (1591, 2382).

Black Pyramid

Anankaia's Temple

Description

Anankaia's Temple is a dedicated monument built by Hermenengild, their Divine Servant. The Temple houses a lectern containing an excerpt of the book Nine Days to Anise, referenced within Objective 4. The temple, built with blackstone, is surrounded by lava, magma blocks, gold, and striders which likely mimics the appearance of the Infernal Consequence. 

Players are advised to avoid destroying the temple; UnknownEmrys incurred consequences following an accidental destruction of the temple using a water bucket. 

The temple can be found near the town of Absenstice at the coordinates (2275, -1268). Located adjacent to it is Hermenengild's Statue. 

Images

Hermenengild's Statue

Description

Hermenengild's statue is a self-portrait depicting themselves, a Divine Servant of Anankaia. In front of the statue, players may utilize a lectern to propose deals to Hermenengild in exchange for a price, usually a valuable item of some sort. On the base of the statue is an inscribed poem, and a chest where players may leave offerings. 

Players are advised to avoid stepping on the statue, as AbleShazbot was ordered to be killed for doing so.

The temple can be found near the town of Absenstice at the coordinates (2275, -1268). Located adjacent to it is Anankaia's Temple.
