General knowledge of Dialian gods

  • Gods can come to be in 3 ways:
    1. “gods that always have been”
    2. “gods that had to be and then were”
    3. “gods that became and knew mortality before”
  • A god’s symbol(s), physical representation, written description, and cultural acceptance vary by location and denomination.
  • Beyond the Great 8, Gods are classed as Greater, Intermediate and Lesser.
