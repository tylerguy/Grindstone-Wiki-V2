Solution

Whenever the numbers with similar symbols are put back to back, you are given:

| 8..4 | 7..9 | 8::? | 7--2 | (84, 79, 8?, 72)

If the unknown number is presumed to be 2, this translates to TORH, which unscrambled creates the name ROHT, the Dialian god of war. This follows previous puzzles’ pattern of the answer being a Dialian god (specifically one of the Great Eight) and is currently the best guess we have for the answer to puzzle 3. The submission box opened later that same day we found the answer.
