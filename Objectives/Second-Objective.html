Overview

Throughout this Objective, applicants were tasked with locating a set of four letters throughout several different Phases. The task necessitated locating several hidden codes both on the old version of the official Grindstone website and EXT-111 Minecraft Simulation. 

Applicants collected the letters “F, K, R, and I," rearranging them to spell the name of one of The Great Eight Gods, Frik. Applicants were able to submit this name on the Grindstone website's Second Objective page.

Second Letter - K

After this information was discovered, applicants received an email from Janet introducing the Second Objective.
 

Upon calling the provided phone number, applicants received a second letter, “K.”

Phase 2 

Third Letter - R

Second Objective Webpage Update

On August 30th, applicants received an update to the task from the desk of Janet in the form of an e-mail. 
 

On the Grindstone webpage dedicated to the Second Objective, an addendum to the original text appeared:
 

Dear applicant,

 We are pleased to inform you that the second phase of mission two is now live. This new mission is different from any previous objectives you’ve been assigned. 

We decided to send you into the world of [REDACTED] where the Universal Magical Academy at Dial has previously [REDACTED]. This is simply a extraplanar *simulation* utilizing a medium in your world.

We have left your first clue at the bottom of this page.

Good luck.

At the bottom of the page, applicants found a long string of text encrypted in binary.1 When converted to ASCII, the binary led to a string of Base64.

EXT-111 Minecraft Server

Applicants were granted permission to the Minecraft server, which is a functioning simulation of the plane EXT-111.2 Within the world appeared large, abandoned cities and journals containing information appearing to be from past researchers. Additionally, players would occasionally receive interactions from mysterious and invisible entities denoted “Watchers”.3

Near the spawnpoint, at the in-game coordinates (319, 2166), applicants found a book placed on a lectern containing information about a “Black Pyramid.”

Applicants began searching the world for evidence of this monument, and were eventually guided there by the help of mysterious and invisible entities denoted “Watchers.”3

Book 1

On August 30th, applicants connected to the EXT-111 server were led by a Watcher to a book containing the first number in a set of coordinates, “42.275111170264"

Book 2

On August 31st, applicants were led by Watchers to a location containing a structure which resembled a ruin, located at the in-game coordinates (5460, -817). They further directed players to a pool of water, inside of which was a tunnel that led to a maze.

Upon completing the maze, applicants discovered a small room containing a lectern and book. The contents of the book revealed the second coordinate within the pair, -83.00806456528959.

Using the coordinate pair (42.275111170264, -83.008064565289959), applicants located a toy shop within EXT-918 named “Toys'R'Us", correctly inferring the solution to be the letter “R." 

Fourth Letter - I

The Black Pyramid

Near the spawnpoint, at the in-game coordinates (319, 2166), applicants found a book placed on a lectern containing information about a “Black Pyramid.”

Applicants began searching the world for evidence of this monument, and were eventually guided there by the help of a Watcher on August 31st. 

The Black Pyramid is located at the in-game coordinates (1601, 6003), buried within a mountain near a river. Upon entry into the cave, applicants battled Watcher-spawned enemies as they climbed a large black pyramid. 

Entering the pyramid through a hole at its peak, they discovered a three-story maze buried below it. Completing the maze led applicants to a small, circular room containing a lectern. 

The book contained a string of binary 01101001 that, when converted to ASCII, revealed the fourth and final letter: “I"

Combining the four letters together and reordering them reveals the answer to the Second Objective, “FRIK"
