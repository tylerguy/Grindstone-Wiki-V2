Homeland

  • When discussing Geography, “Homeland” refers to UMAD's location and the land mass it resides on.
    • It is not the only known land mass (continent)
  • The world hasn't been discovered in its entirety and much of the information such as maps, is Need to Know/Classified
    • Travelling far from the west coast is impossible due to a large, violent and eternal storm
    •  Only recently made contact across waters to the east, similar to ocean between the Americas and Europe
  • Accuracy of maps is not unlike 17th century cartography, though slightly better. 
  • Countries gain their names through impression and phonic ears of those who experience them.
